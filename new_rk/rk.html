    R.K.Township Group is one of the leading Real Estate Companies in the States of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana with five branches located in major cities like Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Rajahmundry , Gajuwaka & Vishakapatnam. The Company establised in 1992 by Sri M.M.kondaiah has acheived great heights in the field of Real Estate under his dynamic leadership. Presently the Company is one of the topmost companies in Real Estate sector in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana States.

    R.K Township Group over the years has sucessfully completed more than 150 Ventures across the states of Andhra Pradeshand Telangana gained confidence of its customers as well as Marketiing Teams & Employees, Every Venture completed by the organisation stands as a testimony to the image of the firm. Quality developments and guaranteed protection for indiviual plots in each of the venture has won the R.K Township Group an enviable reputation.
